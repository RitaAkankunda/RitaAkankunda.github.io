Campus Hustle: Building for Students

How I developed a platform to help students find opportunities.

December 20, 2025

Campus Hustle started as a solution to a common problem: students struggling to find part-time jobs and internships on campus. As a student myself, I saw the need for a centralized platform.

Using React for the frontend and Node.js with Express and MongoDB for the backend, I built a full-stack application that connects students with opportunities. Features include job postings, application tracking, and user profiles.

The project taught me valuable lessons in user-centered design, database management, and deploying applications. It's been rewarding to see how it helps fellow students navigate their careers.
